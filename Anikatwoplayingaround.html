About Our App

Simply Gifts is a application for phones and laptop that helps you find the perfect gift for your loved ones.
By entering a person personality, likes, and previous purchases, the app calculates the perfect gift choice for that person
Filters such as price ranges, star amounts, and sales only convience the users on their search
Ads from our sponsers will play periodically, but can be skipped with our premium subscription
In addition, premium grants a look at what trends daily, weekly, and monthly, and increases the catalog
